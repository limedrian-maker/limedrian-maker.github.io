01 — about

I'm a computing student at the Institute of Computing, Davao del Norte State College (DNSC), where most of my recent work has centered on one question: how do you prove a document — or a system — hasn't been quietly altered?

That question became DocuChain, my capstone project: a blockchain-based document integrity system built for LAN deployment on physical campus servers. Alongside it, I'm CTO of I-Tech, a small team building a 2D pixel RPG designed to actually run on the low-spec hardware common across Southeast Asia, Latin America, and Africa — not just the machines game studios test on.

I like guided, step-by-step problem solving over guesswork, and I tend to build in public with my team rather than alone.
